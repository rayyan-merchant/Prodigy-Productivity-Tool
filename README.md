# Prodigy

A focused productivity workspace combining tasks, Pomodoro sessions, habit tracking, hydration monitoring, analytics, and AI assistance.

## Features


- **Tasks**: Manage tasks with priorities, due dates, and subtasks
- **Pomodoro Timer**: Focus sessions with customizable breaks and session history
- **Habits**: Track daily/weekly habits with streaks
- **Water Tracker**: Log hydration and get personalized insights
- **Analytics**: View your productivity trends and weekly AI insights
- **AI Assistance**: Daily brief, hydration insights, and productivity recommendations
- **Profile**: Customize your profile with avatar and bio
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile devices
